At **BioData 2022** I presented **COMPASS**, Maze Therapeutics' informatics
framework for translating genetic insight into therapeutic targets. Built on a
data-lake architecture, COMPASS integrates tens of billions of records from
diverse sources so researchers can move rapidly from genetic variant to drug
candidate.

I also introduced **Sightline**, a portal for visualizing the R&D portfolio, with
an initial focus on analyzing allelic series to strengthen target validation
across Maze's pipeline programs.
