At **D4 Global 2020** I showed how Maze Therapeutics uses knowledge graphs and
semantic technologies to identify and validate drug targets by analyzing genetic
modifiers — the "protective modifiers" that let some people resist disease despite
carrying disease-causing genes.

I walked through three use cases served by a single cloud-based knowledge graph:
bioinformaticians publishing analysis results, biologists evaluating targets with
structured evidence, and chemists gathering compound information — each integrating
proprietary cohort data with public databases to lower the barrier to analysis.
