Presented to the **NIH Commons Working Group (2016)**, this talk described how
NCANDA enables neuroimaging data sharing by developing standard terminologies, data
models, and APIs in partnership with the neuroimaging community.

We extended the **Neuroimaging Data Model (NIDM)** to support cognitive and imaging
measures, augmented datasets with ontologies, and published them to the Synapse
repository — and explored collaborations with other NIH Commons projects on data
governance, provenance tracking, and digital-object compliance.
