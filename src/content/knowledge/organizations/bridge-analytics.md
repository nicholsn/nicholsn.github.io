# Bridge Analytics

Bridge Analytics builds the Bridge Analytics Environment (BAE), a shared data
and analysis platform that supports open, collaborative research across central
nervous system (CNS) conditions under the CNS Quest initiative. Its focus is
scalable, user-centered biomedical data systems that unify clinical, imaging,
genomics, and sensor data for neuropsychiatric and neurodegenerative research.
