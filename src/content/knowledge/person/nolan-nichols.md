I design and scale biomedical data platforms that enable ambitious science
programs to move faster, integrate deeper, and deliver real impact. As
[Head of Platform](../roles/bridge-analytics-head-of-platform.md) at
[Bridge Analytics](../organizations/bridge-analytics.md) — working under the
CNS Quest initiative — I lead the development of next-generation neuroscience
data infrastructure supporting complex, multi-modal research across
neuropsychiatric and neurodegenerative conditions.

My work sits at the intersection of informatics strategy, platform engineering,
and scientific enablement. I partner closely with computational scientists,
clinicians, and program teams to build secure, interoperable environments that
unify clinical, imaging, genomics, and sensor data into cohesive analytic
ecosystems.

Across nearly two decades in biomedical data, my experience spans human brain
imaging, clinical trials, population-scale genetics, and advanced therapeutic
platforms. I am particularly focused on how structured knowledge, semantic
models, and AI can transform the usability and secondary value of complex
biomedical data — building platforms that don't just store and process data,
but actively accelerate discovery, collaboration, and translational impact.

## Core expertise

- Biomedical platform strategy and architecture
- Large-scale neuroscience data integration
- Knowledge graphs and semantic interoperability
- Secure, governed research environments
- AI-enabled scientific workflows
- Cross-functional technical leadership
