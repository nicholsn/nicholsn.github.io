# Bridge Analytics Environment (BAE)

BAE is the shared data and analysis platform I lead as Head of Platform at
Bridge Analytics — supporting open, collaborative CNS research under the CNS
Quest initiative. It unifies clinical, imaging, genomics, and sensor data into
secure, governed, user-centered analytic environments for neuropsychiatric and
neurodegenerative research.
