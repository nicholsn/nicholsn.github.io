# The Brain Imaging Data Structure (BIDS)

A *Scientific Data* paper introducing the
[Brain Imaging Data Structure](https://doi.org/10.1038/sdata.2016.44) — a
simple, community-driven standard for organizing and describing neuroimaging
data. BIDS grew out of the same open-neuroscience and data-sharing community as
the [Neuroimaging Data Model](../projects/nidm.md).
