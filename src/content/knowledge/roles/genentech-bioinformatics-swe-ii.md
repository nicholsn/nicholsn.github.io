# Bioinformatics Software Engineer II, Genentech

*January 2017 – July 2019 · South San Francisco, CA*

data-release processes for a large-scale clinical-genomics database of
clinical-trial patients with whole-genome sequencing. Developed a platform
supporting reproducible science by unobtrusively capturing data, code,
provenance, and metadata for computational-biology workflows.
