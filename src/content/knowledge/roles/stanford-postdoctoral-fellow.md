# Postdoctoral Fellow, Stanford University & SRI International

*January 2015 – October 2016 · Menlo Park & Palo Alto, CA*

Worked with an interdisciplinary team to design and implement an informatics
framework streamlining brain research on adolescent development (NCANDA):

- Developed an open-source data platform on the scientific-Python stack (Pandas,
  Matplotlib), with automated builds via Docker and continuous integration
  (Travis, CircleCI).
- Harmonized imaging (DICOM) and clinical (Access, CSV, ASCII) datasets into a
  data warehouse for scientific analysis.
- Organized an annual brain-imaging hackathon to promote best practices and
  software adoption.
