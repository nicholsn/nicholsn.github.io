# Graduate Research Assistant, University of Washington

*September 2009 – December 2014 · Seattle, WA*

Doctoral research on reproducibility in human brain imaging:

- Co-founded and collaboratively developed an open data-exchange standard to
  harmonize and integrate brain-data repositories using semantic technology
  (the Neuroimaging Data Model, NIDM).
- Built a distributed-computing framework to track data provenance in
  brain-imaging workflows using NIDM, grounded in the W3C PROV ontology.
- Extended the Foundational Model of Anatomy (FMA) ontology to capture white-
  matter connectivity in the human brain.
- Deployed clinical and imaging data-management applications for the Integrated
  Brain Imaging Center.
